Our Story

Invented by people
who saw the problem

NinoMed was founded on a straightforward observation: OR teams were spending valuable time wrestling with inadequate patient positioning equipment — taping, stacking foam wedges, rigging arm boards — when they should be focused on the patient.

The Safe-T-Secure® was the answer. A single, all-in-one solution that gets a patient safely secured in Trendelenburg position in under a minute, with no shoulder blocks, no tape, and no improvisation. It became the standard at major academic medical centers nationwide.

From there, NinoMed expanded its mission — developing the PneumoHub™ to streamline insufflation management, the STS-BodyPad™ line for a full range of positioning needs, and OR Analytics™ to help health systems recover the billing revenue their EHRs quietly miss every day.

Company History

From one idea
to a full platform

2010s
Safe-T-Secure® launches
The original All-In-One Trendelenburg positioning solution is introduced. Adopted quickly by laparoscopic and robotic surgery programs at leading academic medical centers, including Harvard Medical School's Mount Auburn Hospital.
Mid 2010s
Pluma-Soft® expands the lineup
NinoMed introduces Pluma-Soft® pressure ulcer prevention products, addressing the full scope of OR table patient protection — from positioning through head-to-toe coverage.
2019
OR Analytics™ business intelligence
NinoMed expands beyond medical devices with OR Analytics™ — a revenue cycle analytics platform designed to help health systems identify and recover billing opportunities that standard EHRs miss.
2020–2024
STS-BodyPad™ line and PneumoHub™
The STS-BodyPad™ family expands to cover every clinical scenario — Regular, Regular Plus, XL, and XL Plus — alongside the PneumoHub™ for integrated CO2, smoke evacuation, and scope cleaning management.
2026
120-day biodegradable STS products
NinoMed introduces biodegradable versions of the Safe-T-Secure® and STS-BodyPad™ — a significant step toward sustainable OR practices without any compromise on performance.
