How It Works

Three steps to
captured revenue

Step 01
Connect to your data
Safe-T-Net integrates with your existing EHR, billing system, and OR management software — no data migration, no downtime. Works with Epic, Cerner, Meditech, and others.
Step 02
Safe-T-Net finds the gaps
Proprietary algorithms cross-reference procedure codes, documentation, and billing records to identify missed charges, undercoded procedures, and payer discrepancies in real time.
Step 03
Your team acts on it
Every flagged item arrives as an actionable task — assigned to the right person, with the context they need to resolve it. Dashboards track recovery progress in real time.
Platform Capabilities

Built for revenue
cycle teams

Safe-T-Net is designed around one goal: getting your team the right information at the right moment.

Real-Time Opportunity Detection
Flags missing charges within minutes of case closure — not days later when correction windows have closed. Integrates with OR scheduling to compare performed vs. billed procedures.
Surgical Service Line Analytics
Drill into performance by service line, surgeon, OR, or payer. Identify which procedures have the highest correction rate and why — and track improvement over time.
Payer Performance Tracking
Compare payer reimbursement rates against contractual obligations. Surface systematic underpayments across payers so your contracting team has evidence to act on.
Workflow Task Management
Converts every identified opportunity into a task — routed to the right team member with priority, context, and deadline. Tracks resolution and reopens items that slip through.
Executive Reporting
One-click board-ready reports showing recovery totals, trend lines, and opportunity pipeline. Exports to PDF or integrates with your existing BI tooling.
